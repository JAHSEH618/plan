# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal employees participating in an AI competition — team members (15 groups) and 6 architecture mentors. They reference this document throughout a 10-week sprint to check deadlines, rules, mentor responsibilities, weekly deliverables, final submission requirements, and evaluation criteria. Their context: fast-paced, deadline-driven, mostly reading on desktop during work hours with some mobile access via WeChat.

## Product Purpose

The single-source-of-truth execution plan for a 10-week internal AI competition ("AI 会战"). Teams build AI-powered solutions for real business needs. This document answers every operational question: when are milestones due, what does each week demand, what do mentors own vs. teams own, what counts as a complete submission, and how evaluation works. Success means zero ambiguity — a participant can find any answer in under 10 seconds.

## Brand Personality

Pure utility, zero flair. The document is a tool — like a precisely formatted technical manual. Craft lives in the clarity, not in decoration. The visual reference is Apple's frosted-glass design language (depth through translucency, clean typography, considered whitespace), but every visual choice serves function: glass surfaces communicate information hierarchy, not mood.

## Anti-references

- **Startup pitch decks**: No gradient heroes, no SaaS metric cards, no "changing the world" energy. No decorative color bursts.
- **Government circulars**: No dense unbroken walls of text, no bureaucratic numbering systems, no red-stamp formality.
- **AI-generation clichés**: No gradient text, no side-stripe border accents on cards, no identical icon+heading+text card grids, no tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity above all.** A participant scanning for a deadline must find it in seconds. Hierarchy, spacing, and typography do the work — not decoration.

2. **One source of truth.** Every fact appears once, in one place. No duplication, no ambiguity, no "as mentioned above."

3. **Structure is the interface.** Section numbering, milestone lists, deliverable checklists — the layout itself carries meaning. Glass layers signal importance tiers, not aesthetic preference.

4. **Depth serves function.** Frosted-glass surfaces create visual hierarchy (primary content forward, supporting notes back). Every blur and translucency maps to an information layer.

5. **Restraint earns trust.** No motivational language, no exclamation marks, no color for color's sake. The document's authority comes from precision, not persuasion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance: ≥4.5:1 body text contrast, ≥3:1 large text contrast
- Semantic HTML with proper heading hierarchy and ARIA labels
- Keyboard-navigable with visible focus indicators
- Print stylesheet for paper distribution
- Mobile-readable layout for WeChat sharing (responsive to 375px)
- System font stack (SF Pro Text / PingFang SC) — no custom font loading, instant render
